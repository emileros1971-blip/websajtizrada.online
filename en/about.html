About us

websajtizrada.online is a small, focused studio for web development and online advertising. We're based in Palić (Subotica) and work with clients across Serbia.

We believe in a simple formula: a good site + a clear goal + smart advertising = concrete results. No middlemen, no hidden costs, no cookie-cutter solutions.

We've worked with restaurants, event venues, HVAC companies, construction firms, auto services, cleaning services and plumbers — real projects are visible in the portfolio.
