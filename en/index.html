FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Answers to common questions.

How much does a website cost?
Websites start at 6,000 RSD (~50 EUR). Final price depends on pages, features, content and complexity. Call us for a precise quote.
What's included in the starter offer?
Custom professional design, mobile-responsive version, basic SEO setup, contact form, click-to-call phone and email buttons, domain for 1 year, hosting for 1 year and website publication.
How long does it take?
A simple site can be ready in a few days once content is ready. Complex sites and shops take longer.
Do you build multilingual sites?
Yes. Multilingual sites with proper translations and per-language SEO structure.
Do you run Google Ads and Facebook ads?
Yes. We set up and manage Google Ads and Meta Ads campaigns focused on calls, inquiries and sales.
Do you help with domain and hosting?
Yes — domain and hosting for the first year are included in the starter offer. You can later keep the site at any provider.
